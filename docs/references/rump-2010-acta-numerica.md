Where [johansson-2017-arb](johansson-2017-arb.md) argues ball arithmetic from
an implementation, Rump's survey lays out the whole verification methods
landscape from first principles, including the cost models under which inf-sup
and mid-rad each dominate. When the estate records its division of labor
between `interval-1788` (inf-sup, 1788 semantics) and pfloat-ball (mid-rad),
this survey is the neutral ground the argument should cite. Author copy on the
TUHH page fetched live (3.1 MB PDF); fresh Wayback saves were rate limited on
2026-06-11; the recorded snapshot is pre existing and recent.
