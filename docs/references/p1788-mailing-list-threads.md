The messages and what each grounds, all under
`grouper.ieee.org/groups/1788/email/`:

- Post ballot clause numbering (February 12 2015): msg08075, msg08077, msg08078.
  These place the two output division at clause 12.12.3 in the approved text,
  the numbering the D8.4 draft carried at 12.12.4. Archives:
  web.archive.org/web/20181125132106 (msg08075),
  web.archive.org/web/20181125132105 (msg08077),
  web.archive.org/web/20181125132104 (msg08078).
- Reverse mode debate (May to June 2014): msg07786, msg07819. These record the
  discussion that merged the reverse mode elementary functions into the
  standard's operation battery, the context for why the one output reverses
  take the generic non arithmetic trv rule while the two output division keeps
  its own subclause. Archives: web.archive.org/web/20181125130608 (msg07786),
  web.archive.org/web/20181125131001 (msg07819).

Weight claims by what the source could not have seen: mailing list messages are
working discussion, not normative text, so they witness intent and numbering
history rather than the final clause wording. On the numbering they corroborate
the D8.4 to final move (12.12.4 to 12.12.3); the normative decoration rule lives
in the draft clause text ([p1788-d8-4-draft](p1788-d8-4-draft.md)).
