The reliable_computing mailing list is where the IEEE 1788 standardization was
argued in public; its archives are the primary record of why the standard says
what it says. Load bearing sub pages, each with its own snapshot:

- Mailing list archive: `https://interval.louisiana.edu/reliable_computing.archive/`
  (archived 2025-12-09, web.archive.org/web/20251209191447).
- Mailman list page: `https://lists.louisiana.edu/mailman/listinfo/reliable_computing`
  (archived 2023-12-02, web.archive.org/web/20231202060107).

Fresh Wayback saves were rate limited on 2026-06-11; the recorded snapshots are
pre existing and recent. When the standardization story gets written for the
manuals program, this site is the source to mine; the registry entry exists so
the URL set survives the personal page that hosts it.
