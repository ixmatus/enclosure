The profile exists because full 1788 conformance is heavy; 1788.1 carves out
what most users need. The vendored ITF1788 vectors
([itf1788-framework](itf1788-framework.md)) exercise exactly the binary64 inf
sup territory 1788.1 covers, so the practical conformance story for a narrow
v1.0 would cite this profile, those vectors, and the gaps recorded on the
vector entry. The full standard is [ieee-1788-2015](ieee-1788-2015.md).
