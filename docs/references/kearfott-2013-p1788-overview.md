Kearfott chaired the IEEE P-1788 working group; this overview records the goals
and the decision style of the group from inside, before the standard froze. For
a reader who cannot buy IEEE 1788-2015, this paper plus
[pryce-2016-forthcoming-1788](pryce-2016-forthcoming-1788.md) and
[revol-1788-introduction](revol-1788-introduction.md) reconstruct most of the
standard's intent; for the manuals program it is the standardization story's
primary witness. Preprint fetched live (HTTP 200, 173 KB) from the
interval-computations site ([interval-computations-site](interval-computations-site.md));
the IEEE Xplore version (document 6608444) is paywalled. Fresh Wayback saves
were rate limited on 2026-06-11; the recorded snapshot is pre existing.
