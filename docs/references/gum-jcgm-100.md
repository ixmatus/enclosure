The GUM defines how measurement uncertainty is expressed and propagated in
practice (standard uncertainty, combined uncertainty, coverage factors). An
affine form's noise symbols are a natural carrier for GUM style uncertainty
budgets with correlations kept, which is the design bet the downstream
Enclosed type makes. When that connection gets written up, the citation chain
starts at this entry. The free PDF was fetched live (1.9 MB) and its copyright
statement extracted verbatim; despite free availability the reproduction
terms forbid vendoring, so the entry is pointer plus archive. Fresh Wayback
saves were rate limited on 2026-06-11; recorded snapshots are pre existing
(the publications index page at web.archive.org/web/20260603192850).
