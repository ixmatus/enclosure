Reliable Computing is where much of the interval and validated numerics
literature lives, including the Messine extended affine forms line and the MPFI
paper. The journal survived its publisher: when the Springer print run ended in
2008 the community took it over as a free electronic journal, which makes it
durable in spirit and rot prone in hosting (volunteer infrastructure). The
journal index page and `https://www.reliable-computing.org/` (archived
2026-01-11, web.archive.org/web/20260111055704) are the pointers worth keeping.
